Multi-Level Menu Navigation Systems How to manage sub-categories

How do you handle designing a navigation system when a site has an absurd amount of pages, and those pages have sub categories that have more sub categories within those pages? I know...just breathe.

Cell phone providers' and e-commerce websites are notorious for having information buried in sub pages you will spend way too long looking for.

An interesting case is Intuit's website, which has sub menus buried within sub-menus. This lab is an exercise to simplify the path to purchase, and help the user and/or potential customer stay focused and know where they are at all times, using breadcrubs and smooth interaction design.
